About Us

We are Michelle Tan and Gaby Williams.We met at the girls who code(AppNexus)program in New York and bonded over our love for video games and game design.So naturally we teamed up and made Commute a game focused on bringing up the issue of racial privilege. We believe that games can serve as a platform to bring up important issues as well as a platform for bring joy and entertainment.

About the Game

Commute is a game that shows how racial differences affect peoples lives. It shows the commute of two characters that you can switch between in any part of the game. We chose the names Allison and Aaliyah Johnson because it shows how a "white" or "black" name affects how society sees you as a person.
